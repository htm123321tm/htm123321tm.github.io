Curry comet

My unpopular opinion on this stage is that 1 crown difficulty is the hardest. Yes, you heard that right. That's because at that time, players are unlikely to acquire good anti-relic units to counter Mr.puffinton apart from primordial cat, making the stage really frustrating to play.

The easiest tactic here is probably cyberstacking (presented on the image above). However, this method is very time-consuming and if you are careless and make the cyberpunks die, you have to do everything all over again.
