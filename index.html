Battle Cats Basics

The Battle Cats is a tower defense game where you send weird cats to defeat even weirder enemies. This site is a small guide with simple tips and notes.

Useful Early Units

  • Tank Cat – cheap meatshield to protect your damage dealers.
  • Axe Cat – higher damage close-range unit.
  • Bird Cat – good for early floating enemies.
  • Dragon Cat – long range damage, core carry in early game.

Good Stages to Farm

  • XP Stages – always do the highest XP stage you can clear.
  • Story Levels – replay easy chapters to level basic cats.
  • Treasure Farming – aim for superior treasure in early chapters.

Quick Tips

  • Level basic cats to at least level 10 before rolling too many gacha.
  • Spam meatshields instead of sending one expensive cat alone.
  • Save Cat Food for important draws or inventory upgrades.
