Into the Future – Moon (Chapter 1)

This is one of the first truly scary stages many new players face. Alien enemies appear in large numbers, and careless pushes can wipe your whole lineup.

Why it’s memorable

The boss deals heavy damage and has high knockback, forcing you to use proper meatshields, long-range damage, and sometimes anti-alien units. Players often remember this stage as the moment they realized they couldn’t just spam random cats anymore.

Basic tips

  • Bring at least 2–3 cheap meatshields to stall the front line.
  • Use long-range attackers like Dragon Cat or better gacha units.
  • Save money early, then push when the boss is exposed.
